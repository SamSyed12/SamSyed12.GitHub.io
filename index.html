About Me

I've always liked to get to bottom of what's really happening. At times this manifests itself in hours spent perusing the internet for eye-opening works of journalism, and on other days I simply sit down and sift through a dataset. Finding out what's going on under the hood is both thrilling and satisfying; not only do I like the challenge of finding relationships other's haven't yet noticed, but to me it's an act of getting closer to what's true. I spend the remainder of my time working on fun web/app design, reading short stories, playing football (soccer for my american friends), and travelling whenever/wherever I can.
